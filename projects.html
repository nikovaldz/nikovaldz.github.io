Screenwriting

Shrouded

Shrouded is a feature-length psychological horror script currently in revision.

A grounded, atmospheric screenplay that blends psychological tension with surreal horror, Shrouded explores the fragile boundary between delusion and reality. As a young woman reconnects with her estranged, schizophrenic father, she begins to question the world around her—and herself.

Film

Deadline

A sleep-deprived writer spirals into paranoia—until comfort arrives in the form of a disturbingly kind neighbor.

I wrote, directed, and edited this short film. It is currently undergoing final color correction. While it received runner-up for Best Sound Design at the 2024 Lightworks Film Festival, its greatest strength lies in its psychological tone and minimalism.
